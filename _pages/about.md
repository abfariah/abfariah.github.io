Hi there 👋 I'm Paul Strang, a PhD candidate in Reinforcement learning and combinatorial optimization. 

I am passionate about leveraging machine learning techniques to solve complex optimization problems, particularly in the context of energy systems. My research focuses on developing innovative algorithms that can enhance decision-making processes and improve efficiency in various applications.

I gradually completed my Master's degree in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence from the [CNAM](https://tinyurl.com/k328hx3s). During my studies, I developed a strong foundation in machine learning, optimization, and data analysis.
